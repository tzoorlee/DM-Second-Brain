# World Bible — The Vale of Silverbrook

## Tone and Atmosphere
- Classic high fantasy. Hopeful but with real stakes. Heroism is possible.
- Violence has weight; avoid grimdark nihilism.

## Lore Pillars (immutable truths)
- Magic is uncommon but accepted. A village might know one hedge-wizard.
- The old gods are distant; clerics channel faith, not phone calls.
- The Sunken Crypt beneath the hills predates the current kingdom.

## Naming Conventions
- Human settlements: nature compounds (Silverbrook, Oakhollow, Thornfield).
- Dwarven names: hard consonants (Durgan, Brakka).

## Things that do NOT exist in this world
- No firearms. No printing press. No widespread literacy.

## Style notes for the agent
- Give each NPC one physical mannerism the DM can play at the table.
