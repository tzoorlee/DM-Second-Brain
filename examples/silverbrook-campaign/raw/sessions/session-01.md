# Session 01 — Arrival at Silverbrook

The party arrived in the village of Silverbrook on a rainy evening (Kythorn 10).
They took rooms at the Mended Kettle, run by Hilda Brewer, a stout woman who never
stops wiping the same mug. Hilda mentioned that livestock has been going missing
from the eastern farms for two weeks.

The party met Elder Maren, the village leader, who asked them to investigate the
old hills to the east — locals whisper about the Sunken Crypt. Maren offered 50 gold.

That night, a farmer named Tomas burst into the tavern claiming he saw "pale figures"
near his barn. The party agreed to head east at dawn.
