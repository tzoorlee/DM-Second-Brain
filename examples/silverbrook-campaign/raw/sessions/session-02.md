# Session 02 — The Sunken Crypt

The party tracked the missing livestock to the entrance of the Sunken Crypt in the
eastern hills (Kythorn 12). Inside, they found bones animated by a weak necromantic
presence. They fought through skeletons and discovered a defaced altar.

Behind the altar, scratched into the stone, was a symbol: a broken crown. The party
recovered a tarnished silver locket from the altar. They did not yet understand its
meaning, but Elder Maren went pale when they described the broken crown symbol on
their return.

The livestock raids stopped. Silverbrook is grateful. Hilda gave them free lodging.
