# Chronology of Silverbrook

> Append-only. Each entry dated in the in-world calendar.

## [1492-Kythorn-10 | session-01]
- The party arrived on a rainy evening and lodged at [[The Mended Kettle]].
- [[Elder Maren]] hired them to investigate livestock disappearances near the eastern hills.

## [1492-Kythorn-12 | session-02]
- The party cleared the [[Sunken Crypt]]; the livestock raids stopped.
- A [[Tarnished Silver Locket]] was recovered; the broken crown symbol unsettled Maren.
