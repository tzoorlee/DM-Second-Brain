# The Mended Kettle

The tavern and inn at the heart of [[Silverbrook]], run by [[Hilda Brewer]]. Warm,
low-beamed, and smelling of barley stew. The party's home base in the village.

## Current State
- Open and lively. The party has free lodging here after session 2.

## Notable Features
- A large hearth; the common room where most village gossip is exchanged.

## History / Chronology
- Session 1: the party took rooms here; [[Hilda Brewer]] first mentioned the missing livestock.
- Session 2: free lodging granted as thanks for clearing the [[Sunken Crypt]].
