# Silverbrook

A small farming village in a green vale, named for the silver-bright stream that
runs through it. Rainy, quiet, and — until recently — unremarkable.

## Current State
- **Current government**: led by [[Elder Maren]], informal village council
- **Situation**: at peace again after the crypt raids stopped (session 2)
- **Atmosphere**: grateful to the party, cautiously hopeful
