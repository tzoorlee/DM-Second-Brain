# The Broken Crown

> **DM ONLY.** The PCs do not know any of this yet. This is the campaign's main plot.

## The Secret
Three generations ago, the royal line of the Vale fell — their sigil was a crown,
shattered in the coup that ended them. [[Elder Maren]] was a young servant of that
court and fled to [[Silverbrook]], hiding her past.

The necromancy under the [[Sunken Crypt]] is the dormant will of the last king,
slowly reassembling itself. The [[Tarnished Silver Locket]] holds the last queen's
portrait — and is a key to waking him fully.

## How it should unfold
- The broken crown symbol is the breadcrumb (PCs have seen it — session 2).
- Maren is the reluctant guide who knows the truth (still hidden).
- The [[Order of the Broken Crown]] (prepared, not yet introduced) wants the king back.
