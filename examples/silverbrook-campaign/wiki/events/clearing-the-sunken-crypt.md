# Clearing the Sunken Crypt

## What Happened
- The party fought through animated skeletons in the [[Sunken Crypt]] and reached
  a defaced altar bearing a broken crown symbol.

## Who Was Involved
- The party, the undead of the crypt

## Consequences
- Livestock raids around [[Silverbrook]] stopped.
- The [[Tarnished Silver Locket]] entered the party's hands.
- [[Elder Maren]] was visibly shaken by the broken crown.
